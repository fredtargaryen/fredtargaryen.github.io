Minecraft Mods

I have been making mods with Minecraft Forge since around 2013 and they are my most successful projects. Click the links for more information:

  • Floocraft

    • Brings the concept of Floo Powder, from the Harry Potter franchise, into Minecraft
    • Players build fireplaces to teleport to and from, which are connected to the Floo Network using Floo Signs
    • Floo Powder, when thrown into fire, turns it green and allows the user to walk in and teleport, or peek into the fire and see what's on the other side
    • Floo Torches are torches which emit green light and cause random short-range teleportation on contact
    • Floower Pots consume Floo Powder to automatically transform nearby fires in fireplaces

  • Fragile Glass and Thin Ice

    • Normal glass blocks in Minecraft can't be broken through by running, falling or projectiles. This mod can change that
    • Fragile Glass blocks are made with sugar water in a special cauldron
    • Thin Ice blocks generate in patches on frozen water and break underfoot if an entity is moving too fast
    • Weak Stone patches generate on cave ceilings and crumble into gravel on contact
    • Users can use config files to configure:
      • What entities can break blocks on collision
      • How fast such entities need to be travelling to break them
      • What blocks can be triggered on collision
      • How fast an entity needs to be moving to successfully collide
      • The behaviour of the block on collision (not limited to breaking!)

  • Rocket Squids

    • Rocket Squids are uncommon and spawn in water in the overworld
    • The explosive properties of their ink can propel them great distances
    • The Whistling Conch, a playable instrument, can be found on seashores
    • Rocket Squids have a growing lore and set of interactions with different items - experimentation is encouraged

  • ExRollerCoaster (port for Minecraft 1.12.2)

    • Adds new rails which bend at any angle in any direction, and the coaster follows them
    • Rails have various properties such as branching, redstone acceleration, invisibility or maintaining constant coaster velocity
    • The player camera twists and turns with the coaster (this is work-in-progress in the port)
